F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ship chandling and how do you support vessels?

Ship chandling involves supplying essential goods and services to ships. We provide everything from fresh & dry provisions, bonded stores, and engine parts to safety equipment and onboard inspections.

Do you offer marine engine and electrical repair services?

Yes, we perform electrical work on all types of panels, cranes, compressors, fire alarms, and also offer workshop services for motor rewinding and steel fabrication.

Can I order fresh water or diesel oil for my vessel?

Absolutely. We supply fresh and drinking water, lubricant oils (all grades), marine diesel oil, and gas oil directly to your vessel at port.

What safety equipment and inspections do you provide?

We offer annual inspections for life rafts, CO2 systems, firefighting gear, rescue boats, and also supply nautical charts, safety signs, and FFA equipment.

Do you stock spare parts and mechanical items?

Yes. We supply engine spares, cylinder liners, O-rings, copper washers, mechanical seals, gaskets, valves, and tools, ensuring operational continuity for your ship.

Are emergency delivery and port coverage available?

We serve all major Indian ports and provide emergency deliveries 24/7. Our fast, reliable service ensures minimal delay to your vessel’s operations.

What other products do you supply?

We offer deck supplies, pilot ladders, lashing gear, marine paints, hatch cover tape, cleaning machines, cabin items, medicines, stationery, and printer cartridges.
