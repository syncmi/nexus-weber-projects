Fresh, Frozen & Dry Provisions

We offer a complete range of fresh, frozen and dry provisions that are stocked in our own warehouses containing state-of-the-art refrigeration and freezer facilities. Fruit, vegetables and dairy products are purchased and delivered daily from the market.
