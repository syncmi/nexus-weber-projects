LUMI ULTRA SERIES LED

Brilliant & Energy-Efficient Outdoor LED Displays

  • Operation below 60°C, without AC:
    Ensures stable performance and reduces cooling costs.

  • Up to 50% energy saving:
    Achieved through advanced power management.

  • High brightness up to 10,000 nits with less brightness degradation:
    Guarantees vivid visuals even in direct sunlight.

  • Aluminum cabinet and shell with excellent heat dissipation:
    Provides robust protection and extends lifespan.
LUMI FIT SERIES LED

Versatile & Resilient Outdoor LED Video Walls

  • Aluminum profile & shell with efficient heat dissipation:
    Ensures stable operation in varying temperatures.

  • Automatic moisture removal:
    Protects components from condensation and humidity.

  • Wall-mounted and floor-standing available:
    Offers flexible installation options for diverse outdoor settings.

  • Aviation plug for easy installation:
    Simplifies setup and reduces installation time.

    Offers superior dust and water resistance for outdoor reliability.

  • Up to 50% energy saving:
    Contributes to lower operational costs.
