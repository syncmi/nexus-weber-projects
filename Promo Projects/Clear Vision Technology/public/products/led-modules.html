LED Modules

Clear Vision Technology provides high-quality LED modules, serving as the essential foundation for building vibrant custom LED displays, impactful digital signage, and innovative lighting solutions tailored to your exact specifications.

INDOOR LED MODULE

Precision-Engineered Indoor LED Modules

  • Advanced pad bonding with high stability:
    Ensures enhanced longevity and consistent performance.

  • PC+30 fiber bottom case kit, ensuring robustness and flatness:
    Contributes to a seamless and durable display surface.

  • High grade ICs with a broader color spectrum and evenly divided gray scales:
    Delivers stunning visual clarity and rich, accurate colors.

  • Optimized PCB design:
    Ensures peak performance and efficiency of the module.

OUTDOOR LED MODULE

Robust & Weather-Resistant Outdoor LED Modules

  • Resistant to moisture, UV, and temperature changes:
    Ensures reliable operation in diverse outdoor conditions.

  • High strength to improve flatness and splicing:
    Contributes to seamless and large-scale outdoor displays.

  • Reducing glare for better contrast:
    Provides clear and vivid visibility even in bright sunlight.

SOFT LED MODULE

Flexible & Adaptable Soft LED Modules

  • Splicing with 120-degree flexibility for both concave and convex bending:
    Enables the creation of stunning curved and uniquely shaped displays.

  • Magnetic column improves the stability of installation:
    Simplifies setup and ensures secure mounting for complex designs.
