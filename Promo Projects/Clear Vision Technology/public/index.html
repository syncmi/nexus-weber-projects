What is the ideal pixel pitch for indoor vs outdoor LED walls?

For indoor applications, pixel pitches typically range from 1.2mm to 2.5mm for high clarity at close distances. Outdoor screens usually require 3.9mm to 6mm pitches to maintain brightness and durability in varying lighting.

Can I customize the size and shape of my LED wall?

Absolutely! Our LED displays are modular and fully customizable. We tailor the dimensions, shape, and mounting configuration based on your space and design requirements.

How is an LED video wall different from a regular display?

LED video walls are built with individual LED panels, offering seamless scalability, higher brightness, wide viewing angles, and superior performance compared to TVs or projectors — especially in large or bright spaces.

Do you provide installation and technical support?

Yes, we offer complete end-to-end service including site inspection, design, professional installation, and post-installation maintenance through AMC plans and responsive tech support.

What kind of content can I display on an LED wall?

You can display videos, images, live feeds, data dashboards, promotional content, and more. LED walls support multiple inputs including HDMI, USB, and real-time streaming with content management systems.
